# inspiration for the site's design

after the recent discord security concerns (still ongoing, the dumbshits at discord do not care about keeping your information secure), i got the inspiration for this site's design from [vmfunc.re](https://vmfunc.re/) because they were amongst the ones to find out about Persona's links to Discord's recent controversy.

---

would like to thank them for inspiring me to make a page of my own.

the aesthetic of the late 90s and early 2000s web — **dark backgrounds**, **monospace fonts**, **terminal-green text** — felt like the right choice for what i wanted to build. something that feels *personal* and *intentional*, not corporate and templated.

> if you're reading this and don't have a personal site yet — make one. it doesn't have to be fancy. it just has to be *yours*.
